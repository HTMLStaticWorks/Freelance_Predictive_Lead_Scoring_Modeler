How We Build Your Model

A structured, data-science led approach to predictive modeling.

1
Historical Data Audit

We analyze your last 24 months of winning and losing deals to identify the baseline conversion signals hidden in your CRM.

2
Feature Engineering

We transform raw data points into predictive features (e.g., converting 'Location' into 'Timezone Fit' or 'Title' into 'Buying Power Index').

3
Training & Hyperparameter Tuning

We train multiple models (XGBoost, Random Forest, etc.) and perform rigorous cross-validation to ensure high AUC scores and zero overfitting.

4
Deployment & Feedback Loop

The model is deployed via API to your CRM. We monitor initial performance and retrain the model as new sales outcomes are recorded.
